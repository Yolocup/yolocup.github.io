Tourney rules:

- Yolocup is an osu!standard 1v1 tournament, with a winners and losers bracket.
- Anyone who is not restricted is free to enter.
- Mappoolers may not enter the tournament. Other staff, including hosts, commentators, referees and streamers, may participate. Hosts will not be shown the map pools until they are announced, to prevent cheating.
- All maps will be played with scorev2 and NoFail.
- Based on the number of entries, qualifiers will be played, followed by possible playoffs based on the seed coming out of the qualifiers.
- The number of rounds and points required will be based on the number of entries.
- Dates are still to be announced.
- Reschedules are fine as long as a referee can be present.

Match rules:

- A referee creates a lobby 10 minutes beforehand, after which players receive an invite. Players can join up to 10 minutes after the start of the match. If no good reason is given for absence, the party present automatically wins.
- If both players are not present after 10 minutes, this is considered a forfeit from both sides.
- Players may each choose a warmup map under 4 minutes before the start of the match (not mandatory).
- After warmups, both players roll to determine the pick/ban order. The winner of the roll may pick 1st/2nd pick/ban first; the loser may pick 1st/2nd of what remains. Banning is not mandatory.
- You may pick the same mod multiple times in a row, no problem.
- In case of an (exact) equal score, the map will be replayed.
- For freemod: allowed mods are HD, HR, EZ and FL, combining is allowed.
